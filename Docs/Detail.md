# 🛡️ SuRaksha Hackathon Details
*An initiative by Canara Bank*

## 📝 Overview
**SuRaksha** is a campus-focused hackathon designed to reshape the future of digital security in fintech platforms. As banking digitizes rapidly, traditional password-based logins are becoming insufficient.

This hackathon calls for innovative behavior-based user authentication systems that:
- Enable real-time fraud and anomaly detection.
- Support password-less login mechanisms.
- Ensure a seamless user experience while upholding privacy and convenience.

### Data Privacy Focus
The hackathon addresses data privacy in modern financial services. Safeguarding sensitive customer data across interconnected ecosystems is critical. Participants are encouraged to propose solutions that fortify security and promote transparency, ethical handling, and user trust.

---

## 🎨 Themes

### 1. Real-Time Anomaly Detection
How can a bank automatically detect tampering, changes, or forgery attempts across land records, legal documents, and financial statements in real time? The goal is to provide intelligent insights to support faster, reliable decision-making during underwriting.

### 2. Agentic Regulatory Intelligence & Compliance
Build an **Agentic system** that:
- Monitors regulatory changes.
- Translates changes into "Measurable Action Points" (MAPs).
- Assigns them to the correct bank departments.
- Autonomously validates completion.
